# Market Research Skill

## Purpose
Conduct market research including TAM/SAM/SOM analysis, trend identification, user demographics, and market opportunity assessment.

## When to Use
- Before building a new product
- Validating product-market fit
- Investor pitch preparation
- Strategic planning

---

## Instructions

### 1. Define Market Scope
- What market are we researching?
- Geographic scope (global, regional, local)
- Time horizon (current, 3-year, 5-year)

### 2. Market Sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM)

| Level | Definition | How to Calculate |
|-------|------------|------------------|
| **TAM** | Total Addressable Market | Total worldwide revenue if 100% market share |
| **SAM** | Serviceable Addressable Market | TAM Ã— % we can realistically serve (geography, segment) |
| **SOM** | Serviceable Obtainable Market | SAM Ã— realistic market share (1-5% for new entrants) |

### 3. Data Sources

| Source Type | Examples | Use For |
|-------------|----------|---------|
| Industry Reports | Gartner, Forrester, IBISWorld | TAM, trends |
| Government Data | Census, BLS, Statista | Demographics, economics |
| Competitor Data | SEC filings, press releases | Market share estimates |
| Surveys | Industry surveys | User behavior |
| Job Postings | LinkedIn, Indeed | Demand signals |
| Search Trends | Google Trends | Interest patterns |

### 4. Trend Analysis
- Growth rate (CAGR)
- Emerging trends
- Disruption signals
- Regulatory changes

### 5. User Analysis
- Demographics
- Psychographics
- Behaviors
- Pain points
